How to use this BMI calculator

Enter your height and weight in metric or imperial units. BMI is calculated as weight ÷ height².

  • Under 18.5: underweight
  • 18.5 to 24.9: normal range
  • 25 to 29.9: overweight
  • 30 and above: obese range
